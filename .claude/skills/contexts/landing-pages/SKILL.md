## Landing page context

Landing pages are single-goal conversion surfaces.

Every design decision should serve the conversion goal:
- Sign-up, purchase, download, contact, or demo request
- Above the fold must communicate: what it is, who it's for, why now
- Visual hierarchy flows from hero → value proposition → proof → CTA
- Trust signals (logos, testimonials, stats) reduce friction

Common frame sizes: 1440px wide (desktop), 390px wide (mobile)

Key zones: hero, value props, social proof, features, final CTA

Load context files for format-specific guidance.
