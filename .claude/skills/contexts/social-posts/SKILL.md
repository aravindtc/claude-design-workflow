## Social post context

Social media posts are high-competition, thumbnail-scale, single-message units.

They are seen in a feed at a glance. The design must:
- Communicate the primary message within 1–2 seconds
- Work at thumbnail size (typically 300px on screen)
- Have a single dominant element that anchors attention
- Drive one clear action or impression

Common formats: 1080×1080 (square), 1080×1350 (portrait), 1080×1920 (story)

Load `heuristics.md` for format-specific visual guidance.
Load `variation-types.md` for the full catalog of valid variation types.
Load `anti-patterns.md` for what to avoid.
