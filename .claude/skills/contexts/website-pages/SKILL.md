## Website page context

Website pages serve multiple goals simultaneously: brand, information, trust, and conversion.

Unlike landing pages, website pages must:
- Fit within a navigation system
- Serve users at different stages of awareness
- Support browsing and scanning, not just conversion
- Carry editorial and brand quality consistently

Common frame sizes: 1440px wide (desktop), 1280px (compact desktop), 390px (mobile)

Typical page types: Home, About, Product/Service, Blog, Contact, Pricing

Load context files for page-type specific guidance.
