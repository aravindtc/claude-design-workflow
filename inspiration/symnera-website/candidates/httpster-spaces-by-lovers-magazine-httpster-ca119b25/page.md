## Credits

Spaces is a platform for hiring great designers and engineers. The website copy claims this is more than a job board, letting you show off your company’s culture and values through editorial storytelling.

This website leads with a carousel of video and imagery that feels unique due to the layout and masking of the elements within it. Large white type on a solid black background gives this design its confidence. The large font size shows off the cuts in the typeface [PolySans](https://wearegradient.net/polysans/?ref=httpster.net). In digital design we have no such thing as ink bleed so it’s nice to see typefaces featuring inktraps like this used as a stylistic choice. Scroll based animations add visual flair.
