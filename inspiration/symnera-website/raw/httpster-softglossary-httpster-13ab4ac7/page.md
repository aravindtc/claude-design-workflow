## Credits

.softGlossary is an introduction to the basics of web programming and its creative possibilities. The design is minimal and driven by typography. ASCI art is used in the feature illustration and dot-matrix influences add subtle textures throughout — the ASCI spinning globe is interactive (drag it). Stylised tabs across the top add depth and reference physical media.
