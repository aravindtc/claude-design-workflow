# Approval Flow

Present creative directions for review and iterate based on feedback.

## Steps

1. **Present Options** — Show each creative direction with its rationale and a visual preview
2. **Collect Feedback** — Gather specific feedback on what works, what doesn't, and what to explore further
3. **Refine** — Adjust selected directions based on feedback, combining elements across directions if requested
4. **Finalize** — Lock in the approved direction(s) for production

## Feedback Guidelines

- Ask for feedback on specific axes (layout, color, typography) rather than general impressions
- When a direction is rejected, clarify whether the issue is with the concept or the execution
- Track approved decisions to avoid revisiting settled choices in later rounds
